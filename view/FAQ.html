How to use

  1. Click on Emi tab at top left corner.
  2. Fill the form.
  3. Click "Update" button.
  4. Edit/update any "Installment" section you want and recompute profit/loss and click "Update".
  5. "Difference in Interest" section shows above profit/loss with respect to default EMI amount.
  6. Click "Reset" when you want to clear all history.
